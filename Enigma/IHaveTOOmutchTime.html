Tutorial: How to Play

Welcome to Mathematical Musical Enigmas! In this game, solve mathematical enigmas to discover musical notes.

1. Click "Generate New Enigma".

2. Read the enigma and input your answer.

3. Click "Check Answer" to verify.

4. Correct answer? Congratulations! Try again if incorrect.

5. Use "How to Play" for assistance.

How to Relate Numbers to Musical Notes

It's easy! Each number corresponds to a musical note. For example:

  • 1 = C
  • 2 = D
  • 3 = E
  • 4 = F
  • 5 = G
  • 6 = A

So, when you see a number in the enigma, you can think of the corresponding musical note!
